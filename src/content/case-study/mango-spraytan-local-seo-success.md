## Our Approach: A Comprehensive Search Strategy

We developed a multi-layered approach that addressed every aspect of their online visibility, combining traditional search engine optimization with modern AI optimization techniques. ### Building the Local SEO Foundation

We started by establishing a strong local SEO foundation, which is essential for any service-based business that serves customers in a specific geographic area. Local SEO differs from traditional SEO because it focuses on appearing in location-based searches and Google's local map results. When someone searches "spray tan near me," Google needs to understand not just that Mango Spraytan offers spray tan services, but also where they're located, their hours, their contact information, and their reputation.

We optimized and claimed their Google Business Profile, ensuring all business information was complete, accurate, and consistent across all platforms. This included adding high-quality photos of their spa facilities, setting up proper service categories, and establishing their service area. We implemented consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across all online platforms, which is crucial for local SEO because search engines use this consistency as a trust signal.

Through comprehensive local keyword research, we identified high-intent search terms that potential customers were actually using, such as "spray tan near me," "luxury spray tan [city]," and "spa services [city]." We then optimized their website content to match these search queries, creating location-specific landing pages for different neighborhoods and areas within the city. This approach helps capture searches from customers in various parts of the city, not just their immediate neighborhood.

On the technical side, we ensured their website was mobile-first, as the majority of local searches happen on mobile devices. We optimized page load speeds, implemented proper heading structures with location information, created comprehensive XML sitemaps, and ensured secure HTTPS connections. We also built citations in local business directories and secured listings in spa and wellness-specific directories, which helps establish local authority and improves rankings in local search results.

### Creating Content That Answers Customer Questions

Content strategy for local businesses isn't just about keywords—it's about answering the questions potential customers have at every stage of their decision-making process. ### Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): Preparing for AI-Powered Search

Answer Engine Optimization, or AEO, represents a fundamental shift in how search works. Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in search results that users click through to visit websites. AEO, however, optimizes content to be selected as the source for AI-generated answers that appear directly in search results, voice assistant responses, and AI-powered answer engines.

The importance of AEO has grown dramatically as AI-powered search has become mainstream. When someone asks Google "Where can I get a spray tan near me?" or asks Siri "What's the best spray tan salon in [city]?", AI systems need to extract information from web content to provide direct answers. If your content isn't optimized for this extraction, you miss out on this visibility entirely.

For Mango Spraytan, we optimized their content specifically for voice search and conversational queries. People don't type "spray tan services" the same way they speak it—voice searches are more natural and question-based. We structured content in question-answer formats that AI systems can easily extract, and we implemented FAQ schema markup that helps search engines understand which content answers which questions.

We also targeted featured snippets, which appear in "position zero" above the regular search results. Featured snippets are often read aloud by voice assistants, making them crucial for voice search optimization. By creating concise, direct answers to common spray tan questions and structuring content with clear headings and bullet points, we increased the likelihood of Mango Spraytan appearing in these prime positions.

Perhaps most importantly, we ensured their content appears in Google AI Overviews, which are AI-generated answer summaries that appear at the top of many search results. When AI systems generate these overviews, they typically cite only a few authoritative sources. By optimizing Mango Spraytan's content to be one of those cited sources, we ensured brand visibility even when users don't click through to the website.

### Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): Building Long-Term AI Authority

Generative Engine Optimization, or GEO, takes optimization a step further by ensuring content is not just cited by AI systems, but actually learned from and synthesized into AI knowledge bases. This is particularly important for long-term visibility because generative AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, and Google Gemini don't just retrieve information—they learn from it during training and create new content based on what they've learned.

The difference between AEO and GEO is subtle but important. AEO focuses on being cited in current AI-generated answers when the AI has access to the live web. GEO focuses on being part of the AI's training data and knowledge base, ensuring visibility even when the AI isn't connected to the internet. For a business like Mango Spraytan, this means that when someone asks ChatGPT "What are the best spray tan salons in [city]?" months from now, the AI might reference information it learned from Mango Spraytan's content during training.

We enhanced the semantic richness of Mango Spraytan's content, creating comprehensive coverage of spray tan services that provides deep, meaningful information rather than just surface-level descriptions. AI systems learn better from content that provides context, background, and clear relationships between concepts. We structured content logically with clear headings and semantic HTML, making it easier for AI systems to understand and learn from.

We also built authority signals that AI systems recognize—demonstrating expertise through detailed service descriptions, showcasing credentials and certifications, and establishing trustworthiness through consistent, accurate information. AI systems are designed to prioritize authoritative, trustworthy sources, so building these signals ensures better visibility in generative AI responses.

The multi-platform visibility we established means Mango Spraytan's content is discoverable by ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, and various AI research agents. When these systems learn from or synthesize information about spray tan services, Mango Spraytan's content becomes part of that knowledge base, creating long-term visibility that compounds over time.

### Understanding the Crawler and AI Bot Activity Metrics

One of the most telling indicators of successful optimization is the significant increase in crawler and AI bot activity that we observed. This metric might seem technical, but it's actually a powerful leading indicator of search optimization success.

![Mango Spraytan Metrics - Crawler and AI Bot Activity](/assets/portfolio/MangoMetrics.png)

When we talk about "crawlers," we're referring to automated programs that search engines and AI systems use to discover, read, and index web content. Traditional search engines like Google use crawlers (like Googlebot) to continuously explore the web, reading websites and adding them to their search indexes. The frequency and depth of crawling indicates how important search engines think your content is.

The dramatic increase in Googlebot activity we observed means Google's crawler was visiting Mango Spraytan's website more frequently and exploring more pages. This is a positive signal because search engines prioritize crawling websites they consider valuable and authoritative. When a website is crawled more frequently, new content gets indexed faster, and the website appears in search results more quickly.

But what's particularly interesting is the significant influx of AI bots we observed. These aren't traditional search engine crawlers—they're AI systems actively discovering and learning from the content. OpenAI's GPTBot (used by ChatGPT) was indexing content for AI training and knowledge base inclusion. Anthropic's AI systems (used by Claude) were discovering and learning from the content. Perplexity's crawler was actively gathering information for real-time AI search results. Google's AI systems (including Gemini) were indexing content for AI Overviews.

This AI bot activity is significant because it indicates that the AEO and GEO optimization strategies are working. AI systems only crawl and learn from content they consider valuable, authoritative, and well-structured. The fact that multiple AI systems are actively discovering Mango Spraytan's content means the optimization for AI understanding is successful.

What this means for the business is substantial. When AI systems learn from your content, you gain visibility in AI-generated responses even when the AI isn't connected to the live web. This creates long-term visibility that compounds over time. The content becomes part of the AI's knowledge base, meaning future queries about spray tan services might reference information learned from Mango Spraytan's content.

The increased bot activity also indicates faster content discovery. When we publish new content or make updates, both traditional search engines and AI systems discover and index it more quickly. This means the business can respond faster to trends, seasonal changes, and market opportunities.

Most importantly, this level of AI bot activity is uncommon—most competitors aren't seeing this level of AI system engagement. This gives Mango Spraytan a competitive advantage because their content is being learned from and synthesized by AI systems while competitors' content is not.

## Key Takeaways: Lessons for Other Businesses

This case study demonstrates several important principles for businesses looking to improve their online visibility. First, local SEO is essential for service-based businesses—it's not optional. When customers search for local services, they expect to find businesses in their area, and if you're not optimized for local search, you're invisible to those customers.

Second, multi-platform optimization maximizes visibility. By combining SEO, AEO, and GEO, Mango Spraytan achieved visibility across all search platforms. Traditional SEO drives direct traffic through search rankings. AEO captures visibility in AI answer engines and voice search. GEO ensures long-term visibility in generative AI systems. Together, these strategies create comprehensive search presence.

Third, reputation management directly drives both search visibility and customer conversion. Positive reviews improve search rankings, and they also build customer confidence. The two work together—better rankings bring more customers, more customers bring more reviews, and more reviews improve rankings further.

Fourth, content quality matters across all platforms. Well-structured, informative content ranks well in traditional search, gets cited by AI systems, and becomes part of AI knowledge bases. There's no shortcut—quality content is the foundation of all search optimization.

Fifth, effective local SEO can expand your service area without opening additional locations. As Mango Spraytan became more visible in search results, customers from across the city began traveling to their location. This geographic expansion happened organically through search visibility.

Finally, a comprehensive search strategy creates sustainable, long-term growth that compounds over time. Unlike paid advertising, which stops working when you stop paying, organic search optimization continues to deliver results and improve over time. The combination of SEO, AEO, and GEO ensures visibility as search continues to evolve.
